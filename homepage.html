    About us

    Founded in 2010, Fresh Paws Spa has been providing exceptional grooming services to pet owners in the local community for over a decade. At Fresh Paws Spa, we understand the importance of keeping pets clean and healthy, and our team of experienced and dedicated groomers are passionate about providing the best possible care to every pet that walks through our doors. We believe that every pet deserves to be treated with love and respect, and we strive to create a comfortable and stress-free environment for pets during their grooming sessions.

    Our pet grooming services include everything from baths and haircuts to nail trimming and ear cleaning. We use only the highest quality grooming products, and we take the time to carefully assess each pet's individual needs to ensure that they receive the best possible care.
